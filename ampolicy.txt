Privacy Policy
Last Updated: 23 July 2026
This Privacy Policy explains how Airline Mayhem ("the App") handles your information.
Your privacy is important. The App is designed to collect as little personal information as possible and does not operate any backend servers controlled by the developer.

1. Information We Collect
The App itself does not require you to create an account or provide personal information.
Any game progress or settings are stored locally on your device unless you choose to use Apple Game Center.

2. Apple Game Center
The App supports Apple Game Center for features such as:
Leaderboards
Achievements
Player profiles
When you use Game Center, Apple may process information including your Game Center nickname, player identifier, achievements, leaderboard scores, and other information necessary to provide Game Center services.
This information is handled by Apple in accordance with Apple's Privacy Policy.
The developer does not have direct access to your Game Center account or personal Apple ID information.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)
The App displays advertisements using Google AdMob.
Google and its advertising partners may collect certain information to provide and measure advertisements, which may include:
Device information
Approximate location (if permitted by your device settings)
Advertising identifiers
App interactions
Diagnostic information
Where required by law, the App requests your consent before personalized advertising is shown.
Depending on your choices and region, you may receive:
Personalized advertisements
Non-personalized advertisements
Google processes this information according to its own Privacy Policy.

4. Analytics
The App itself does not collect analytics.
However, Apple Game Center and Google AdMob may collect diagnostic or usage information necessary for their services.

5. Data Storage
Game progress, settings, and other application data are stored locally on your device unless specifically synchronized through Apple services such as Game Center or iCloud (if enabled by Apple).
The developer does not store your data on external servers.

6. Third-Party Services
The App uses the following third-party services:
Apple Game Center
Google AdMob
These services have their own privacy practices and policies.

7. Children's Privacy
The App is not directed specifically toward children under the age required by applicable law.
If the App is used by children, advertising and consent handling will operate in accordance with applicable platform requirements and local regulations.

8. Your Choices
You may:
Control advertising permissions through your device settings where available.
Reset or limit your advertising identifier using your device settings.
Disable Game Center through your Apple device settings if you do not wish to use Game Center features.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.
Any updates will be published onthe App's website, and the "Last Updated" date will be revised.
